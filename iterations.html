History of Trumpets

Overview | History | Artists | Construction

Throughout its development, there have been many iterations of the trumpet. From earliest to latest they are:

  1. Graves Post Horn (1845)
  2. Cornet or Trumpet in Bb and G by E.G. Wright (1860)
  3. Courtois Trumpets
  4. Boston Trumpets (1887)
  5. Conn Trumpets (1899)
  6. Besson Trumpets aka The Modern Trumpet (1910)
